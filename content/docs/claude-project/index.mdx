Your Claude Project is the party leader. It holds the 5 Core files (meta guide, tool matrix, program overview, day checklist, voice guide), your business brief, and anything else you upload (research briefs, draft outputs, screenshots). Chat memory persists across every chat in the Project. It plans your day, suggests which tool to use for what, and reviews the output when you bring it back.

Free-tier Claude supports Projects. You get up to 5 Projects per account with up to 20 files per chat and a 30MB total. This is all you need for the weekend. It's important you use Claude as the "Brain" and not the execution as you will otherwise quickly hit its free limits. If you want to power up you can always pay the $20 pro subscription.

## What the Project does

- Plans your day. You tell it where you are, it tells you what to do next.
- Routes work. For example, copy drafting goes to ChatGPT. Fact checks go to Perplexity. Images go to Gemini Nano Banana or Ideogram. Back to Claude for review.
- Holds memory across chats. Upload the core files once on Day 1. They persist for every chat you open inside the Project.

## What the Project does NOT do

- Draft your full landing-page copy. That burns your free-tier quota fast. Let ChatGPT or Gemini draft, bring it back to Claude for a red-team read.
- Pick your tools. The tier list is at [Tools](/docs/tools). Claude routes you to them.
