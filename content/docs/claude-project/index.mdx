Your Claude Project is the party leader. It holds your business brief, the tool matrix, and the program overview. It plans your day, tells you which tool to use next, and reviews the output when you bring it back.

Free-tier Claude supports Projects. You get up to 5 Projects per account with up to 20 files per chat and a 30MB total. This is all you need for the weekend.

## What the Project does

- Plans your day. You tell it where you are, it tells you what to do next.
- Routes work. Copy drafting goes to ChatGPT. Fact checks go to Perplexity. Images go to Gemini Nano Banana or Ideogram. Back to Claude for review.
- Holds memory across chats. Upload the core files once on Day 1. They persist for every chat you open inside the Project.

## What the Project does NOT do

- Draft your full landing-page copy. That burns your free-tier quota fast. Let ChatGPT or Gemini draft, bring it back to Claude for a red-team read.
- Pick your tools. The tier list is at [Tools](/docs/tools). Claude routes you to them.
