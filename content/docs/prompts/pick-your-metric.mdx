## Why this works

The single number you're moving this weekend. Anchors the [Day 1](/docs/playbook/day-1) landing page CTA, the Day 2 distribution choices, and the Day 3 raid hour count. Two metrics means zero.

## Prompt

```txt
Here's my 4-box:

[PASTE YOUR 4-BOX FROM PROMPT 2]

I'm running TETR LaunchLab this weekend. Pick the one number I'm moving by Sunday night.

Output:
- The metric name in 2 to 4 words
- Why this one for my shape (2 sentences max)
- A target number that means "this worked" by Sunday (one number, no ranges)

Rules. Pick exactly one. If two metrics seem to compete (sales vs signups, followers vs subscribers), tell me which one is upstream and pick that. The metric has to be countable in real time on Sunday.

If my 4-box is too vague to pick from, ask me one question and stop. Don't guess.
```

## Examples

Six shapes, six metrics. Match yours.

### B2C invite-only (Back Room)

Waitlist signups for the first private drop. Pre-drop list count by Sunday night. Not Instagram followers, not landing-page views. Target. 30.

### B2C e-com (shop open, product live)

Direct purchases. Money in. The clearest signal that the offer holds at the price. Target. 5 paying customers in 72 hours.

### B2B service (consulting, agency)

Demo calls booked. Higher ticket, longer cycle. The signal this weekend is whether someone wants 30 minutes with you. Target. 3 booked.

### SaaS or freemium tool

Trial signups with an activated workspace. Not "I'll try it later" intent. They made an account and used it once. Target. 10.

### Newsletter or creator product

Email subscribers from cold traffic. Friends and family don't count. Strangers who came off your distribution channels and chose to opt in. Target. 25.

### Custom 1:1 outreach (bespoke, beta-style)

Replies or qualified DMs. People who wrote back, not just opens. The intent signal for hand-built sales. Target. 8 conversations.
