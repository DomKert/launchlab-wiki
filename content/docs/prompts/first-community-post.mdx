## Why this works

Day 2 PM is the hardest move of the weekend. You're posting to strangers, not your own list. Voice has to match the venue or you get banned or ignored. This prompt produces one post or one DM tuned for a specific place, plus a "would a mod ban this" check.

## Prompt

```txt
I'm posting to [SPECIFIC VENUE, e.g., r/VintageFashion or DMing a Substack writer @handle] about [PRODUCT in one line]. The venue's rules and tone are roughly [ONE LINE, e.g., "no self-promotion, identification threads welcome, expert-enthusiast register"].

Write me one post or DM for this venue.

If it's a post:
- Title (platform-appropriate length and shape)
- Body (3 short paragraphs, ends with a real question not a CTA)
- One follow-up comment I can leave myself to seed discussion

If it's a DM:
- Subject or opening line
- 3-sentence body that opens with something specific to them, not about me
- Explicit ask or end-state

Voice rules: talk like a member of that community, not a marketer. No "I'm excited to share," no "just launched," no "reach out if interested." If a mod would remove this, flag why and rewrite.

Give me one "safe" variant and one "punchier" variant. Tell me which to send first.
```

## Back Room sample answer (abbreviated)

> **Venue.** r/VintageFashion, 180k, self-promotion banned, ID threads welcome.
>
> **Safe variant (post).** Title: *Help identifying this 1970s Italian knit?* Body: photo of one piece from the upcoming drop, 3 paragraphs on the label, weave, and a guess at provenance. Real question: *"Anyone recognize the maker from that label stitching?"*
>
> **Punchier variant (post).** Title: *I source European vintage for a living. AMA if useful.* Body: 3 paragraphs on how sourcing works, one honest take on why Depop is getting worse, one invitation to ask anything. Soft mention of The Back Room only if someone asks where to find your stock.
>
> **Follow-up comment.** "edit: a few people asked about sourcing ethics, I'll try to answer individually but the short version is I buy directly from estate sales and small shops in 3 countries, no middlemen."
>
> **Ship.** Safe variant first. Punchier version after you've got 2-3 unflagged posts in the subreddit and some built credibility.
