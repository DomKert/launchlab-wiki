## Why parallel beats single

One AI scout gives you one angle. Three give you the contradictions, the gaps, and the consensus. The contradictions are the most useful part because that's where weak claims expose themselves. Gemini's 1M context window collapses all three outputs into one brief so Claude doesn't have to.

## Steps

1. **Perplexity free.** Run the scout query once. Save the output with citations. Spend 1 of your 3 daily Pro Searches.
2. **Grok free** (DeepSearch on grok.com). Same query, different angle. Grok pulls live X sentiment that Perplexity doesn't see.
3. **Gemini free** (Deep Research mode if available, otherwise standard chat with grounding on). Same query a third time. Gemini reaches further into long-tail academic and trade sources.
4. **Gemini free** (new chat). Run [Prompt 15 Synthesize the parallel scout](/docs/prompts/synthesize-parallel-scout) with all 3 scout outputs pasted in. Output is a 500-word brief that surfaces shared learnings, contradictions across the 3 scouts, and 1 to 2 gaps. 1M context handles the full raw dumps in one shot.
5. **Claude Project.** Upload the brief as a Project knowledge file (`research-brief.md`). Now [Prompt 2 The 4-box](/docs/prompts/four-box) and [Prompt 4 Landing page spec](/docs/prompts/landing-page-spec) have rich context to work from across every chat.

## Where else to fan out

The same fan-out-and-compare pattern works on more than research.

- **Landing builder bake-off.** Same spec into 2 builders (Bolt and Framer, or Bolt and Lovable). Ship the one you like the look of, archive the other for ideas.
- **Hero image generation.** Same image brief into 2 generators (Nano Banana and Ideogram). Pick the one that lands the wordmark cleanest.
- **Caption draft.** Same image and post intent into ChatGPT and Gemini side by side. Stitch the strongest sentences from each, run [Prompt 9 Strip AI smell](/docs/prompts/strip-ai-smell) on the merged version.

The synthesis is the unlock. One tool gives you one shape. Several give you the choice.
