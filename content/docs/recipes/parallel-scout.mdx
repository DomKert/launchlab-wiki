## Why parallel beats single

One AI scout gives you one angle. Three give you the contradictions, the gaps, and the consensus. The contradictions are the most useful part because that is where weak claims expose themselves. NotebookLM and Gemini collapse the noise so Claude does not have to.

## Steps

1. **Perplexity free.** Run the scout query once. Save the output with citations. Spend 1 of your 3 daily Pro Searches.
2. **Grok free** (DeepSearch on grok.com). Same query, different angle. Grok pulls live X sentiment that Perplexity does not see.
3. **Gemini free** (Deep Research mode if available, otherwise standard chat with grounding on). Same query a third time. Gemini reaches further into long-tail academic and trade sources.
4. **NotebookLM free.** New notebook. Drop all 3 scout outputs in as sources (paste as text or upload as .txt). Ask grounded questions like "what 3 patterns show up across all three scouts" and "where do these scouts disagree". Citations show which source said what.
5. **Gemini free.** Paste the NotebookLM synthesis. Ask for a 500-word briefing that cuts redundancy and keeps the contradictions. 1M context handles all the source dumps if you also paste the raw scout outputs as backup.
6. **Claude Project.** Upload the compressed briefing as a Project knowledge file. Now [Prompt 2 The 4-box](/docs/prompts/four-box) and [Prompt 4 Landing page spec](/docs/prompts/landing-page-spec) have rich context to work from across every chat.

## Where else to fan out

The same fan-out-and-compare pattern works on more than research.

- **Landing builder bake-off.** Same spec into 2 builders (Bolt and Framer, or Bolt and Lovable). Ship the one you like the look of, archive the other for ideas.
- **Hero image generation.** Same image brief into 2 generators (Nano Banana and Ideogram). Pick the one that lands the wordmark cleanest.
- **Caption draft.** Same image and post intent into ChatGPT and Gemini side by side. Stitch the strongest sentences from each, run [Prompt 9 Strip AI smell](/docs/prompts/strip-ai-smell) on the merged version.

The synthesis is the unlock. One tool gives you one shape. Several give you the choice.
