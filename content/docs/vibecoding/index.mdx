## Why this gets its own section

Vibe-coding is one of two real programming skills the weekend teaches (the other is prompting Claude itself). Every other tool you touch is a one-shot use. Bolt is where you'll spend hours, and it's where the cost spiral actually happens. The discipline matters as much as the tool.
