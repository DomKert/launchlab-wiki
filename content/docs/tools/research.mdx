## Multi-tool routing for research

Claude Project plans. AI scouts (Perplexity, Grok, Gemini) cite and synthesize. Primary-data tools (Google Keyword Planner, Meta Ad Library, Reddit) supply ground truth. NotebookLM is your synthesis layer with per-source citations. Use AI to compress, not to replace.

## Day 1 morning flow

Five concrete moves. AI compresses, you still read primary sources.

1. **Keyword candidates**. Claude generates 20 from a seed term. You validate volumes in Google Keyword Planner.
2. **Demand trend**. Confirm direction in Google Trends. Cross-check Exploding Topics if the curve looks weird.
3. **Competitor ads**. Manual scan in Meta Ad Library. Paste 3 to 5 ad copies into Claude for a pattern summary.
4. **Competitor traffic**. Sanity-check sizing in Similarweb (15/day cap). Falls back to Semrush at 10/day.
5. **ICP voice**. Manual read on Reddit, Quora, X. Synthesize 5 to 10 sources in NotebookLM with citations. Audio Overview while you make coffee.

Then back to your Claude Project for the 4-box.
