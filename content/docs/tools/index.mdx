## Multi-tool routing comes first

Claude thinks. Perplexity and Grok research. ChatGPT and Gemini write. Everything else builds and ships.

You will hit a rate limit on every tool this weekend. The workaround is routing, not paying. Plan in Claude (your Project), hand the right job to the right tool, bring the output back to Claude for review.
