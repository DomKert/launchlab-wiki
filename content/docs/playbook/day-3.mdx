## The Raid Hour

50 students publish inside one 60-minute window. Each brings 8 to 10 genuine engagers from their own warm network.

Squad-plus-friends, not 50-way cross-engagement. Platforms read pod behavior. Real warm networks read different. Wave structure is soft. Squads 1 to 5 first 30 min, 6 to 10 second 30. Post when your network is online.

Reddit excluded. Post 24 to 48h after the Raid Hour from your own karma-built account, 90/10 rule.
